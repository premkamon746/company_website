# Privacy Policy for QR Scanner Pro

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-19
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-19

Mega Innotech ("we", "us", "our") operates the QR Scanner Pro mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and is **never transmitted to us**:

- **Scan history** — the contents and timestamps of QR codes you scan or create
- **Favorites** — items you mark as favorites
- **App settings** — theme, language, vibration, sound, and other preferences
- **Generated QR codes** — images you create and save

You can clear this data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

### 1.2 Information Processed but Not Stored

- **Camera image data** — used in real time to detect QR codes; image frames are processed on-device by Google ML Kit and are not stored or transmitted
- **Imported gallery images** — when you choose to scan from gallery, the image is read into memory for detection only

### 1.3 Information Sent to Third-Party Services

When you use certain features, limited data is sent to third parties:

- **Google Safe Browsing** — when you scan a URL, we may send the URL to Google's Safe Browsing service to check whether it is known to be malicious. This helps protect you from phishing and malware. See [Google's Safe Browsing privacy notice](https://safebrowsing.google.com/).
- **Google AdMob** — we display ads served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See [Google's privacy & terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) and AdMob's [data disclosures](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903).

We do not maintain our own servers that collect personally identifiable information from you.

## 2. Permissions We Request

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Camera | Required to scan QR codes from the camera |
| Photos / Media | Optional — to scan QR codes from images you select |
| Wi-Fi state / change Wi-Fi | To automatically connect when you scan a Wi-Fi QR code |
| Fine Location | Required by Android for the Wi-Fi connect feature; we do not collect or share your location |
| Biometric / Fingerprint | Optional — for the in-app lock feature |
| Internet | For Safe Browsing checks, AdMob, and feature updates |
| Vibrate | For scan feedback |
| Notifications | For widget updates |

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information solely to provide and improve the App's features. We do not:

- Sell your personal information
- Use your scan history for advertising
- Build user profiles for cross-app tracking

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- Locally stored data remains on your device until you clear it via Settings or uninstall the App.
- Third parties (Google) retain data according to their own policies; see their links above.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws such as GDPR (EU/UK) or CCPA (California), including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information. Because we do not collect personal information ourselves, most data is already under your control on your device. For questions about data processed by Google services, please refer to Google's privacy policies.

## 7. Security

We rely on Android's platform security features and use HTTPS for all network requests we make. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.
